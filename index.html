About TidyHouse

TidyHouse Chores started as a small family project to make household responsibilities feel less like a burden and more like a team effort. Instead of nagging and forgotten tasks, we wanted a simple visual system that everyone in the house — kids and parents alike — could check at a glance.

This site is a demo built to explore what a lightweight, no-login, no-cost chore tracker could look like. Every chore below is an example — feel free to imagine it as your own household's list!
